What is this web application for?   Fuzzy Magic lets you do basic fuzzy matching operations on spreadsheets.

What is fuzzy matching?   It is a method for finding duplicate rows in a spreadsheet that are not 100% match.

What are the basic fuzzy matching operations that this web application lets you do?  These are the two basic fuzzy matching operations that you typically need to perform on your spreadsheets:

Detect duplicates

detect duplicate or similar rows in your spreadsheet and sort them so that such "fuzzy" matches are grouped together

Merge spreadsheets

merge two spreadsheets into one so that any two duplicate or similar rows are merged into a single row
